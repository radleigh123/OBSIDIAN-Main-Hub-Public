## Continuing string literals
- lines terminated by a `\`
```C
printf("%s", "You have to dream before your dreams can come true.\
--A.P.J. Abdul Kalam"); // considers 4spaces

printf("%s", "You have to dream before your dreams can come true."
"--A.P.J. Abdul Kalam"); //does not consider the indentation
```
