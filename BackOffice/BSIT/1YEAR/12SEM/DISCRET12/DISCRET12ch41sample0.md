Suppose $x$ is a particular real number. Let $p$, $q$, and $r$ symbolize “$0 < x$,” “$x < 3$,” and “$x = 3$,” respectively. Write the following inequalities symbolically:
**a.** $x \le 3$
$q \lor r$

**b.** $0 \lt x \lt 3$
$p \land q$

**c.** $0 \lt x \le 3$
$p \land (q \lor r)$
