```C
char string1[] = "Hello";
printf("%s\n", string1); // Hello

printf("%s\n", "You have to dream before your dreams can come true.\
--A.P.J. Abdul Kalam"); // Considers the 4 spaces before --

printf("%s\n", "You have to dream before your dreams can come true. "
"--A.P.J. Abdul Kalam"); // Does not consider indentation before --
```
